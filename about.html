The Company Behind

Underwear made with passion

Our manufacturing partner JBS and company behind Adam Underwear was established in 1995 and has become the leading underwear manufacturers in Scandinavia in terms of size, revenue and popularity. They are an international, visionary and financially strong organization with 300 employees, all dedicated to manufacturing the best underwear while focusing on four core values: comfort, quality, innovation, and individuality. We believe that combining these 4 values makes for the ultimate underwear experience, and we’re excited for you to experience for yourself.
